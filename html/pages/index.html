What We Do

OneGlobe provides innovative software engineering, architecture, and IT consulting services to federal and commercial customers. Our engineers engage at all levels from strategy to execution. We create solutions using modern approaches to agile, devops, security, big data, and the cloud. We use these practices to solve problems and deliver outstanding mission success for our customers.

Agile Software Development

We deliver value to our customers by using proven agile techniques and methodologies. With a practical approach, we keep the focus on what really matters: collaboratively delivering high quality software that meets your needs. We recognize that agile delivery requires discipilne as well as flexibility, and can help our customers transition heavyweight processes and systems to more agile, adaptive models.

Quality is at the core of everything we do. Our focus on automated continous delivery, testing, and improvement ensures that we are not only delivering, but delivering with high quality in a repeatable, rock-solid manner.

Cloud Platforms

We build systems that leverage today's scalable cloud platforms. We define our infrastructure as code and use bundled, efficient cloud services where it makes sense. Our architecture and transition teams can help outline cloud strategies and migration plans to move even the most complex systems to cloud or hybrid platforms. OneGlobe is a member of the AWS Partner Network.

Data Engineering

No matter the size or shape of your data, we can help put it to work for you. We build secure and flexible big data platforms, analyitics and visualization engines, and data distribution hubs that take the risk out of data management.

Integration Services

OneGlobe engineers implement software integrations across a variety of disciplines. Our experts are able to deliver solutions that help our customers leverage the latest technology, packages, and platforms.

  • Business Process and Rules Management
  • Identity, Credentials, and Access Management
  • Salesforce and CRM

Technology Partners

We work with a variety of vendors and technology providers to ensure that we can leverage their platforms to the fullest for our customers. Our expertise is recognized through membership in partner programs.


Who We Are

We're software engineers, architects, and consultants. We're multi-talented, customer-focused, and people-centric. We care about our customers and their missions, and we make it a point to bring the best possible solutions. We're good at what we do. OneGlobe is a small business founded in 2007 and is based in Ashburn, VA.
