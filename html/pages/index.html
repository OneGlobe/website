Agile Software Development

We provide value to our customers by developing software the most efficient way possible using proven agile techniques and methodologies. With a practical approach, we keep the focus on what really matters: collaboratively delivering high quality software that meets your needs.

Cloud Architecture & Development

OneGlobe employs highly-skilled architects with hands-on experience using all of the major cloud providers. We can help you plan the right solution for your organization from cloud, on-premise, or hybrid, and deliver the systems to meet your needs.

IT Architecture

Information Technology Architecture and data flows are some of OneGlobe's core service offerings. We handle IT Architecture at all levels of an organization: business, enterprise, system, solution, data, application, software, infrastructure, service, cloud, AWS, Salesforce and more.

Amazon Web Services

Our AWS Certified Solution Architects will determine the right solutions to meet your organization's architecture, security, development, optimization, monitoring, and application services challenges. Whether it's cloud-first or a hybrid footprint, custom-developed or AWS service-based, OneGlobe can efficiently and expertly implement your needs using AWS.

DevOps Engineering

Not just a buzz word, but the way we do business. On-premise and in the cloud, we automate and coordinate your continuous development, integration, and delivery practices to deliver value to you faster.

Business Process Management

At the heart of all organizations are the processes they perform. We can help to define, model, and implement those processes using the latest BPM software tools while meeting audit, monitoring and reporting requirements with the best user interface possible.

Salesforce Implementation

Harness the power of Salesforce using our certified Salesforce Developers to help you configure solutions out of the box, and customize as needed. Our architects and developers can integrate your existing processes into a full-featured Salesforce application.

Identity & Access Management

We deliver identity and access management solutions that support attribute-based, role-based, and policy-based access control systems. We protect enterprise data and systems to meet the security, governance, and audit requirements of today's operational environments.
